The Wiz

Dorothy, a teenage girl living on a farm in rural Kansas, is miraculously transported to an urban fantasy land called Oz while trying to save her dog from a storm. After arriving, she accidentally kills the Wicked Witch of the East and learns of the Wizard of Oz, who can help her return home. On her journey to find the Wizard, Dorothy is joined by the Scarecrow, the Tin Man, and the Cowardly Lion.
